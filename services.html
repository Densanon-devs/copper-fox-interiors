Services

Expert design direction — without the full-service price tag. Copper Fox focuses on consultations and wall treatments, with a trusted network for everything beyond.

[ Design consultation ]
01 — Design Consultations

Design Consultations

Whether you're refreshing a single room, furnishing a model home, or planning a larger project, a consultation gives you expert, personalized direction — layout, palette, materials, and sourcing guidance you can act on yourself or hand to your team.

Ideal for homeowners working through their own projects and builders styling model homes across the Spokane to Coeur d'Alene area.

[ Staging consultation ]
02 — Pre-Listing Occupied Staging Consultations

Pre-Listing Staging Consultations

Staging to sell looks different than decorating to dwell. In an occupied consultation, we walk your home room by room and build a clear plan to de-clutter, de-personalize, and stage with your own belongings — plus a punch list of high-impact updates before you list.

Focused on the rooms that move buyers — kitchens, living rooms, and primary suites — so every effort works harder.

[ Wall treatment ]
03 — Wall Treatments

Wall Treatments

The right wall treatment transforms a room. From accent walls and paint direction to wallpaper selection and placement, Copper Fox brings a designer's eye to the surfaces that set a space's tone.

Wallpaper installation and specialty finishes are handled through Jaclyn's trusted vendor network.

Beyond the Consult

A trusted network

Copper Fox focuses on consultation and design direction. For full staging installs, wallpapering, window treatments, and other branches of design, Jaclyn taps a trusted network of regional vendors and partners — so you get the right specialist for the job, coordinated through one point of contact.
